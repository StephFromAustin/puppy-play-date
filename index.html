Winnie's Birthday Challenge!

How to Play

Help Winnie have a great birthday!

Winnie needs an exact number of puppies to attend his birthday party to apply for the Guinness Book of World Records!

BUT! There's a catch! He can only have the exact random number assigned; NO MORE, NO LESS.

There are four puppy breeds below. By clicking on a puppy you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

To help Winnie, match your total score to the random number assigned. You are unsuccessful if your total score goes above the random number.

HINTS: The value of each puppy is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game restarts the value of the puppy will change.
